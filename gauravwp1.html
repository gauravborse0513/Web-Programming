What is Cryptocurrency?

Cryptocurrency is a type of digital currency that relies on encryption techniques to regulate the creation of new units and secure transactions.

History and Evolution of Cryptocurrency

The first cryptocurrency, Bitcoin, was created in 2009 by an anonymous person or group named Satoshi Nakamoto. Over time, other cryptocurrencies like Ethereum and Litecoin emerged.

How Cryptocurrencies Work

Cryptocurrencies operate on a decentralized network based on blockchain technology. Blockchain is a distributed ledger where all transactions are securely recorded.

Advantages and Disadvantages

  • Advantages: Decentralization, fast transactions, low fees
  • Disadvantages: Volatility, lack of regulation, potential for illegal use
